The Thesis

Vaulted wealth is dead wealth.

Existing fractional platforms lock luxury goods in climate-controlled rooms forever. We think that's a category error. The point of a Rolex is to be worn. The point of a '67 Mustang is to be driven. Tangible is the first platform that lets you do both — generate alpha and live with it.

01 · PROBLEM

The vault paradox.

Alt-asset platforms force you to choose between ownership and enjoyment. A Patek you can't wear is a chart on a screen. We solve for both at once.

02 · SOLUTION

Rotational custody.

Own 25% of a watch? You get 91 days a year with it on your wrist. Smart contracts handle the schedule. Insured shipping handles the logistics.

03 · EDGE

Provenance verified.

Every asset is authenticated by independent experts, GPS-tracked during transit, and insured to full appraised value. Possession is logged on-chain.

Operating Model

Four steps. No vault.

STEP ONE

Source & authenticate.

Our specialists source pieces with documented provenance. Each item is authenticated by an independent panel — three experts must agree before listing.

STEP TWO

Fractionalize & underwrite.

Assets are securitized into 100 shares. Each share grants pro-rata economic exposure and pro-rata physical access. Full-value insurance is included.

STEP THREE

Rotate & enjoy.

Your share % converts to days per year. The schedule auto-allocates based on demand and seasonality. Insured concierge shipping moves the asset to you.

STEP FOUR

Exit on your terms.

Sell shares to other members on the secondary market — or vote with co-owners to liquidate the entire asset at appraisal. Median holding period: 3.4 years.

Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

What happens if I damage the asset?+
Every asset carries comprehensive policy coverage to full appraised value, with normal wear-and-tear allowances calibrated by category. Deductibles vary; for watches, the first $250 of repair per custody period is on the active holder. We've never lost an asset to damage.
How is authenticity verified?+
Three independent specialists must independently authenticate each item. Watches go through movement inspection; cars get a 230-point provenance review; sneakers are X-rayed and chemically tested. Every transfer is logged immutably.
Can I sell my shares early?+
Yes. Tangible operates a continuous secondary market with weekly settlement. You can list any portion of your position at any time. Typical bid-ask spreads run 1.5–3% for liquid assets.
What's the fee structure?+
2% sourcing fee on purchase, 1% AUM annually (covers insurance, storage, and concierge logistics), and a 10% performance fee on realized gains. No fees on rotation custody — that's the whole point.
Is this an SEC-registered security?+
Each asset is offered under Reg A+ or Reg D depending on size and accreditation. Disclosures, offering circulars, and audited financials are published per filing requirements. Tangible is a registered broker-dealer member of FINRA and SIPC.
